## Whats Umbra

Umbra allows you to easly control your pc from your smartphone via WIFI.

The app requires the server running on your pc.

## Features

#### Mouse:
- Movement and click
- Wheel scroll
- Tap to click
- Move with gyroscope

#### Keyboard
- Qwerty keyboard
